# Monthly Campaign Collection

Ten selected campaign visuals covering January through June 2026. The set was chosen
for range rather than one fixed style: corporate, seasonal, playful, retail, tropical,
pixel art, neon, and minimal product presentation.

## Selected images

1. `01-new-year-grand-sale.webp` - clean New Year campaign with a warm family image and clear product offer.
   Source: `012026-Jan/Jan - Double Digit/Lazada/Untitled-2-02.png`
2. `02-february-mid-month.webp` - high-energy red Mid-Month campaign with strong promotional hierarchy.
   Source: `022026-Fab/Shop Campaing Feb-04.png`
3. `03-valentine-mid-month.webp` - pink Valentine campaign combining seasonal graphics with a full product range.
   Source: `022026-Fab/Fab - Mid Month/Lazada/febmid26-01-01.png`
4. `04-summer-health-sale.webp` - long-form summer campaign using a tropical pool scene and marketplace offers.
   Source: `032026-Mar/Mid- Pay relocate.png`
5. `05-shopee-3-3-campaign.webp` - bold orange 3.3 campaign focused on fast offer communication.
   Source: `032026-Mar/Mar - Double Digit/0202-03.jpg`
6. `06-songkran-campaign.webp` - Thai seasonal campaign mixing Songkran imagery, people, and product promotion.
   Source: `042026-Apr/Artboard 1.png`

   Source: `042026-Apr/Apr - Double Digit/Artboard 1 copy.jpg`
8. `08-back-to-school.webp` - playful pixel-art campaign presenting school essentials and hygiene products.
   Source: `052026-May/May - Mid Month/shopee.jpg`

    Source: `062026-Jun/Jan - Double Digit/Artboard 1.png`

## AI disclosure

Codex did not add AI-generated content to images 01-07, 09, or 10 during selection and
WebP export. Image 08 uses AI only for extending the pixel-art school background; its
campaign copy, promotion details, logos, student, and product assets come from the
original layered artwork.
